## Project Overview
一个 AI 增强型自由职业服务市场 MVP。 平台让需求方先通过 Skill AI 低成本“试镜”自由职业者的方法、风格和判断能力，再决定是否提交真人服务意向；响应侧可以发布自己的 Skill AI，用经验、流程、案例和服务边界承接轻量咨询，并把高价值需求转化为线索。 当前项目已实现 Skill 市场、需求雷达、AI 试镜聊天、信任校验舱、线索 CRM、报价单、托管订单模拟、需求侧/响应侧个人中心，以及成交后的履约评价背书。平台定位为展示、试用、撮合和交易辅助工具，不直接承诺 AI 输出或真人服务结果。

An AI-enhanced freelance service marketplace MVP. The platform allows clients to first 'audition' freelancers at a low cost through Skill AI to evaluate their methods, style, and judgment before deciding whether to submit a real service request; on the provider side, freelancers can publish their own Skill AI, use their experience, processes, case studies, and service boundaries to handle light consultations, and convert high-value requests into leads. The current project has implemented a Skill marketplace, demand radar, AI audition chat, trust verification module, lead CRM, quotation forms, escrow order simulation, client-side/provider-side personal centers, as well as post-transaction performance and endorsement reviews. The platform is positioned as a tool for showcasing, trial, matchmaking, and transaction support, without directly promising AI outputs or real service results.
